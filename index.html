Our Mission

At Kindness for Afghans, our mission is to bring hope and relief to the most vulnerable families in Afghanistan—widows, orphans, and households without income. We collect charitable donations from Canada, the USA, and other countries to provide food and essential supplies to those in urgent need.

Our dedicated team operates within Afghanistan, working directly in local communities to identify families in crisis and deliver immediate assistance. This hands-on approach ensures that your contributions reach those who need them most.

Kindness for Afghans is a registered charity in Ontario, Canada.
Ontario Corporation Number: 1001294785

We accept donations via Interac e-Transfer (Canada), Zeffy (Online), and International Wire Transfer. Every contribution, no matter the size, makes a real difference.
