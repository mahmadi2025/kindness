Our Mission

At Kindness for Afghans, we believe in the power of direct action. Our team on the ground identifies the most vulnerable households—widows, orphans, and families without income—and delivers immediate relief.

100% of your donation reaches these families. We have zero overhead for our international operations.

We accept donations via Interac e-Transfer (Canada), PayPal, and International Wire Transfer. Every contribution, no matter the size, makes a difference.
